Medium shot of an enthusiastic presenter at a clean minimal desk, 
slow push-in toward face, bright natural lighting with warm fill, 
clean white background with subtle floating icons, 
shallow depth of field, normal speed, 
modern educational YouTube aesthetic, 4K

```
Top-down bird's eye view of hands arranging colorful infographic elements 
on a bright white surface, static camera with subtle zoom, 
soft overhead studio lighting, high key, vibrant accent colors, 
sharp focus throughout, time-lapse style arrangement, 
clean educational design aesthetic
```

```
Medium close-up of presenter with confident warm smile, 
slight push-in then hold, warm three-point lighting, 
soft background bokeh with blurred bookshelf, 
normal speed, approachable expert tone, 
broadcast quality, cinematic color grading
```

## 负面提示词通用库

```
Negative: blurry, low resolution, distorted, watermark, text overlay, 
bad anatomy, extra limbs, morphing, glitching artifacts, 
overexposed, underexposed, noise, grain (unless intentional), 
shaky camera (unless handheld specified), cartoon style (unless specified),
unrealistic physics, floating objects
```
